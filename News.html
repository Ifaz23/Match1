Xiaomi Mi 11 Ultra Getting MIUI 12.5 Update With System Improvements, Bug Fixes & More

We announced earlier this year a brand new new update for Mi 11 Ultra called MIUI 12.5. This software update will be rolled out to devices in the upcoming months and will bring system improvements, improvements to notes, Xiaomi Cloud and changes to the Status Bar as well as the Notification shade.

This Update comes with build number V12.5.2.0.RKAINXM and is codenamed 'Stable Beta'. In other words, the over-the-air (OTA) update is being rolled out in batches. Hence, it may take some time to reach each and every user in the country. Users of the Xiaomi Mi 11 Ultra can check for the update by going into Settings > System > System Updates. Alternatively users can download the link separately via a link. According to the changelog, the MIUI 12.5 update brings system improvements, improvements to Notes, Xiaomi Cloud, and changes to the Status Bar and Notification shade.

THE NEW UPDATE FOR THE XIAOMI MI 11 ULTRA MAKES THE SNAPDRAGON 888 COOLER

In the case of Xiaomi Mi 11 Ultra, we have seriously invested in our creation, betting on an additional screen and a main camera, which received three new generation Quad Bayer sensors at once. The new smartphone runs the latest Qualcomm flagship processor Snapdragon 888. Its choice was obvious, but We did not manage to overcome the overheating problems. And this, together with the high price tag of the Xiaomi Mi 11 Ultra, somewhat crippled the blissful impression of the flagship.

But MIUI developers have found a way to cool the hot temper of the chip. They did this at the software level by releasing the MIUI 12.21.7.14 update. The first to receive it were the owners of the Chinese version of the Xiaomi Mi 11 Ultra.
